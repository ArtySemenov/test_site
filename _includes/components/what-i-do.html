What I do

UX Design

I craft digital products that are usable, accessible, and intelligent. I am passionate about empowering users to achieve things they never thought possible wherever they are. My process is human centered and data driven, which allows me to create brilliant solutions built based on observation and data.

Graphic Design

I love beautiful things, and I enjoy making them. I work using the Adobe Suite and Autodesk Maya to create designs that grab attention, evoke emotion, and compell to act. I work with local printers to design and deliver any type of printed material, from a simple leaflet to conference collateral.

Digital Art

This broad category covers a wide variety of digital artifacts that I love producing. I paint atmospheric, functional concept designs, render reallistic architectural visualisations, and work in CAD software to design products. I've worked on projects as concept artist, 3D artist, and product visualiser.

Information Architecture

For me, good UX is based on excellent information architecture. How we search for information is largely based on our biases and understanding these biases is what I do. Randing from a few page website to a complicated financial website navigation, I make order with research and human centered design.
